Overview of TGMF. The framework models local Gaussian motion with a second-order Taylor formulation and refines Gaussian–mesh coupling through differentiable motion backpropagation.

Abstract

TGMF explicitly models dynamic Gaussian attributes around a reference state with interpretable velocity and acceleration terms. By coupling local Gaussian motion with a mesh scaffold and optimizing them in a bidirectional loop, the method improves temporal stability while preserving local flexibility under expressive head motion.
